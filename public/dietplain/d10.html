General Guidelines

  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Get regular exercise, aiming for at least 30 minutes most days of the week.
  • Practice portion control to maintain a healthy weight.
  • Avoid excessive consumption of processed foods, sugary drinks, and unhealthy fats.

Overall Well-being

Take care of your mental and emotional health by:

  • Practicing stress-relief techniques such as meditation, deep breathing, or yoga.
  • Making time for hobbies and activities you enjoy.
  • Getting enough sleep each night (7-9 hours for adults).
  • Seeking support from friends, family, or a mental health professional if needed.

Skincare

Keep your skin healthy by:

  • Cleansing your face twice daily with a gentle cleanser.
  • Moisturizing regularly to keep your skin hydrated.
  • Using sunscreen with at least SPF 30 every day, even on cloudy days.
  • Avoiding excessive sun exposure and tanning beds.
  • Eating a diet rich in vitamins and antioxidants to support skin health.

Gut Health

Promote a healthy gut by:

  • Eating plenty of fiber-rich foods like fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and legumes.
  • Including probiotic-rich foods like yogurt, kefir, sauerkraut, and kimchi in your diet.
  • Avoiding excessive intake of alcohol, caffeine, and processed foods.
  • Staying hydrated and drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Managing stress through relaxation techniques and regular exercise.
